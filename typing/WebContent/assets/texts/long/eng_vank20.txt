Myeongdong, Epicenter of Korean Fashion
Myeongdong is the epicenter of Korean Fashion.
In 1956, Korea's first fashion show was held in Myeongdong, marking the beginning of Korea's modern fashion history.
Despite many changes, Myeongdong retains its reputation as a fashion mecca.
It offers diverse shopping choices, including department stores, local and international brand stores, and independent stores.
Fashion leaders from Korea and from around the world are flocking to Myeongdong.
