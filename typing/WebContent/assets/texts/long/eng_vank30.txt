Taste of Korean Food
Korean food = Health food.
From ancient times, Koreans have thought that food and medicine have common root.
Therefore, both the ones who make the food and the ones who eat the food think of health.
When cooking, people balance both animal-nature and vegetable ingredients and use less fats.
In addition, Korean food presents variety of tastes according to different ingredients and recipes.
For example, there are hundreds of types of Kimchi, and numerous kinds of teas.
From now on, enjoy the diverse charm of Korean food. And be a friend of Korea.
