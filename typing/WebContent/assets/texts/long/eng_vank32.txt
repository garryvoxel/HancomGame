Haemul-pajeon Seafood Green Onion Pancake
Haemul-pajeon is a dish made by pan-frying green onion and seafood in mushy dough.
It goes well with Makgeolli, Korean traditional rice wine.
Dongnae-pajeon from the Busan city is the most famous Haemul-pajeon.
It was even presented to the king during Joseon Dynasty(1392~1910).
