Mt. Seorak, Famous Mountain in the Land of Mountains
Mt. Seorak is a famous mountain on the eastern side of Korea.
Korea is a mountainous country where you can see a mountain everywhere.
A geography book of Joseon, published in 1530, explains the origin of the name Seorak "Snow on this mountain continues to melt even through the early summer, so people call it Seorak (meaning Snow Mountain)."
Mt. Seorak is 1708 meters high, the third highest peak in South Korea.
It offers unique scenery during all four seasons, and has many cultural assets and tourist attractions.
