Jeonju Hanok Village, Place for Traditional Korean Experience
Jeonju Hanok Village is a town where over 700 Korean traditional houses (Hanok in Korean) are located.
This village was created to preserve Korean national identity by residents in Jeonju during the Japanese Occupation Period (1910-1945).
Now, it has become the most popular tourist destination in Jeonju to experience traditional Korean culture.
Jeonju is a city where you can experience well-preserved Korean traditions, such as traditional music, dance, and craft.
